### Controls

+ ![left icon]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/sprites/narutoBackward.png)

+ ![right icon]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/sprites/narutoBoost.png)

+ ![charge icon]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/sprites/narutoCharge.png)
